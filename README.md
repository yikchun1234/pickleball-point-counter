🎾 Pickleball Point Counter

A minimalist, Muji-inspired Pickleball Point Counter built with React & Tailwind. Tracks Standard, 14-Point, and Custom rally scoring. Features a visual rally timeline, dynamic court positioning, a smart voice score announcer, local match history, and supports 12 languages. PWA-ready and runs entirely as a single HTML file. Perfect for tracking matches on the go!

✨ Features

* Zen, Muji-Inspired UI: Clean, distraction-free interface with smooth animations and interactive dot-matrix backgrounds. Seamlessly adapts to your device's Light & Dark modes.

* Multiple Rule Sets: * Standard Scoring: 0-0 (Singles) or 0-0-2 (Doubles) rules, play to 11, win by 2.

  * 14-Point Rule: Rally scoring to 15, with an optional "Must Serve to Win" sudden-death toggle at 14-14.

  * Custom Rules: Fully adjust the 'Play To' score, 'Win By' margin, and toggle 'Serve to Score' on or off.

* Smart Voice Announcer & Haptics: Built-in Text-to-Speech acts as your digital referee, announcing the score, second servers, and side-outs out loud using high-quality female voices. Features Smart Language Detection to pronounce international player names natively even if the app is in another language! Subtle haptic vibrations provide tactile feedback during intense matches.

* Court Positioning Tracker: A dynamic, rotating mini-court visualizes exactly who is serving, who is receiving, and which side (Odd/Even) players should be standing on based on the current score and side-outs.

* Advanced Match Controls: Track 2 timeouts per team, prompt players to switch ends at half-points, safely "Undo" mistakes (even after the match ends), and easily "Reset" a match to quickly play again with the same settings or a fresh score.

* Visual Rally Timeline: A beautiful visual heatmap is generated for every match to track exactly how the game unfolded, point by point.

* Export as Image: Instantly generate and share a sleek, Instagram-ready (4:5 aspect ratio) graphic of your final scorecard, complete with a timeline and "Winner" badges directly to your camera roll or social media.

* Match History & Stats Dashboard: Automatically saves completed matches to your browser's local storage. View your overall win rate, average match duration, and review past game timelines.

* Multi-Language Support: Fully translated into 12 languages: English, 简体中文, 繁體中文, Bahasa Melayu, Español, Français, Tiếng Việt, ไทย, 日本語, 한국어, Deutsch, and Português.

* Hidden Arcade Room: Tap the "New Match" title 5 times to unlock a secret retro arcade featuring 8 mini-games (Snake, Pickle Jump, Memory, Tic-Tac-Toe, Reaction Test, Wall Bounce, Tap Challenge, and Target Practice)!

* PWA Ready: Install it directly to your iOS or Android home screen to use it offline like a native mobile app.

* Zero-Build Setup: The entire application runs natively in a single index.html file using CDNs. No Node.js, Webpack, or Vite required!

🚀 How to Use (For Players)

Playing a Match

1. Open the app and tap the screen to start.

2. Select your match format (Singles or Doubles) and your rule set (Standard, 14-Point Rule, or Custom).

3. (Optional) Customize the match title, enter team names, choose who serves first, and toggle Timeouts or Switch Ends.

4. During the game, simply tap the Team 1 or Team 2 buttons to award a point or side-out based on the active rules.

5. Check the rotating court map in the center to confirm server and receiver positions.

6. Made a mistake? Hit the Undo button to reverse the last action.

Viewing History, Stats & Exporting

1. On the main menu, click the History (clock) or Stats (chart) icons in the top right corner.

2. The History drawer reveals all your past matches, final scores, and the complete point-by-point visual timeline for each game.

3. Click the Share icon on any completed match to instantly generate a beautiful scorecard image to save or share with friends.

Installing on your Phone

* Android / Chrome: Scroll to the bottom of the main menu and click "Install as a native app".

* iOS / Safari: Tap the "Share" icon at the bottom of your Safari screen and select "Add to Home Screen".
